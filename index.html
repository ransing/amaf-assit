Asthma Medication Administration Form

  • Successful school asthma management involves individual case identification and provision of medications to students when needed.
  • New York City (NYC) schools require caregiver submission of an Asthma Medication Administration Form (AMAF; a physician-completed order form that must be renewed every school year) to confirm asthma diagnoses and allow school nurses to administer medication for asthma exacerbations.
  • Without an AMAF, due to systems-based regulations, the nurse cannot administer medication, even if a student is having an asthma attack.
